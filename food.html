Bun rieu cua

Crab rice noodles

Crab rice noodles is a Vietnamese traditional dish widely known both domestically and internationally. This dish includes rice noodles and crab soup. Crab soup is a soup made from crab meat, tomatoes, fat, vinegar, fish sauce, salt, spring flowers.

Com ga Hoi An

Chicken Rice

Chicken rice is one of the most favorites when visitors have just arrived in Hoi An. It is a combination of rice, chicken broth and chicken raised naturally.

Goi cuon

Spring rolls

These spring rolls are a refreshing change from the usual fried variety and have become a family favorite. They are great as a cool summertime appetizer, and are delicious dipped in one or both of the sauces.
